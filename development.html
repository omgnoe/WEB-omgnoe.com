Eurorecup Pointage

Eurorecup Pointage

📱 iOS & Android

🔒 Private - Employee Access Only

About

Eurorecup Pointage is a professional time tracking and attendance management system designed specifically for employees. Clock in and out of work shifts (fiche de travail) with ease and access construction site information instantly.

Key Features:

  • Easy clock-in/clock-out functionality
  • Work shift tracking (fiche de travail)
  • Construction site information access
  • Employee-only secure access
  • Real-time attendance management
  • Available on both iOS and Android

Note: This application is privately listed on both the App Store and Google Play Store. Access is restricted to authorized Eurorecup employees only.
